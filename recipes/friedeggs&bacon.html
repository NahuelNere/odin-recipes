ハウルの朝ごはん

(Howl´s Breakfast)

Description

Howl’s Moving Castle is one of my favorite Ghibli films and the breakfast scene with calcifer is probably one of the most popular food scenes out of all the Ghibli films, or even anime in general.

While it’s a very simple Western breakfast, it just looks so darn good while they’re cooking and eating it.

Ingredients, you will need

  • 3 slices thick cut bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 loaf bread
  • 1 slab cheese

Step by Step, How to cook it

  1. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add 3 slices of bacon to the pan and cook until crispy. Push them to one side of the pan.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium. Crack 4 eggs directly into the pan next to the bacon. Cook until sunny side up eggs are to your preference.
  4. Remove the pan from the heat.
  5. Cut desired amount off from the loaf of bread.
  6. Serve the bacon, eggs, bread, and cheese family-style.

Additional Notes

I think most Western households will have all of these items already at home. But the most important thing to note is the BACON.
